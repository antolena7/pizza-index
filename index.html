Project Overview

This project tracks real Pentagon visits and correlates them with spikes in pizza sales in nearby areas. The data visualized here is based on publicly available information and real sales trends.

Historical Correlation

  • September 11, 2001: Major spike in Pentagon security and related local food deliveries.
  • June 12, 2025: Recent escalation involving Israel and Iran, linked with notable Pentagon activity.
